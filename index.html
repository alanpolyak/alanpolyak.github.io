About Myself

I am a passionate team leader, data scientist, developer, and designer interested in creating a meaningful impact on the world through artificial intelligence, data visualization, information ethics, and novel technologies.

I am a student at Cornell University, pursuing my Master’s in Operations Research and Information Engineering, focusing on machine learning and management. As an undergrad at Cornell, I majored in Biometry & Statistics and Information Science. Currently, I am looking for quantitative opportunities in organizations that have strong missions to change the world for the better.

Outside of the workplace, my hobbies are quite eclectic. I am a music producer and urban photographer with a passion for politics and graphic design. I love to study new languages in addition to the five I can speak fluently, and try foreign cuisine as well.
