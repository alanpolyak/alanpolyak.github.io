About Myself

I am a data scientist and algorithmic justice advocate with professional analytics experience in the tech sector, specifically software, sustainability, payments, and hardware industries. Algorithmic justice, to me, means ensuring large-scale ML systems are not hurting marginalized communities, and goes beyond that to alleviate the effects of historical oppression. I take justice for intersectional identities seriously and personally.

I started off my career saving endangered African forest elephants with AI, and ever since, I knew I wanted to dedicate my work to creating a positive social impact through the power of big data. I am looking for product-oriented data science roles that leverage my expertise in analytics and the quantitative decision sciences to assist in data-driven and responsible decision making. I am interested in companies that have strong and authentic missions to change the world for the better.

Outside of the workplace, my hobbies are quite eclectic. I am a polyglot of 5 languages and am currently learning Mandarin, I make AI-generated art, and I brew kombucha with some experimental/funky flavors.
